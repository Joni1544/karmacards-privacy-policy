Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information directly from users.

The App offers optional in-app purchases.

All payments are processed securely through Apple’s App Store payment system.
We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information (such as credit card details).

Apple may collect and process purchase-related data in accordance with its own Privacy Policy.
You can review Apple’s Privacy Policy at:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

3. Data Storage

The App may store gameplay progress locally on your device.

We do not store or transmit gameplay data to external servers.

4. Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use third-party analytics tools.
The App does not use tracking technologies for advertising purposes.
The App does not collect device identifiers for tracking.

5. Third-Party Services

The App relies solely on Apple’s App Store services for payment processing.
No additional third-party services are used to collect user data.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
Since no personal data is collected, the App complies with applicable child privacy regulations.

7. Data Security

Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal information stored on external servers.
